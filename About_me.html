About me:

Previous Next

I am driven by my curiosity and an unsatiated hunger to learn. I am pursuing IBDP Year 2 (Grade XII) at Dhirubhai Ambani International School, Mumbai, studying Physics, Mathematics and Economics as major.

I seek my answers in science. For me, the facts presented by experiments and their theoretical explanations represent the truth. My passion for physics offers me a way to explore the unexplored. I am actively pursuing research in Quantum Physics, having authored a research paper in Quantum Computing. I am also working on a project in Quantum Technology with Indiaâ€™s leading research laboratory at DRDO, Ministry of Defence.

This passion for science has not diminished my quest for understanding the rich culture and tradition that has served us for thousands of years and yet remains the foundation of our social value systems and ethos. It helps me seek answers to the supra-physical and the supra-logical. I enjoy the privilege of reaching out to my grandfather, one of the foremost scholars on the subject. My enriching interactions with him have been documented and are being published as my first book on the subject.

I was initiated into Piano in 2019. I opted for the Western Classical style. While practicing the epic compositions of the masters, I feel swayed away from the realms of this world. The harmonics in the notes and reverberations take me to a journey where I drift away from the sheet music, and wander on my own in a world full of melody.

Having traveled to Finland, Singapore, Hong Kong, China, France, Switzerland, Italy and the United Kingdom, my journey to the world has just begun. I aspire to be a citizen of the world.
