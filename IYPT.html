IYPT

International Young Physicists’ Tournament (IYPT): www.iypt.org
The International Young Physicists’ Tournament (IYPT), often referred to as the ‘Physics World Cup’, is a scientific competition between teams of secondary school students. It mimics the real-world scientific research and the process of presenting and defending the results obtained. Participants work for a year on 17 open-ended inquiry problems involving easy-to-reproduce phenomena presenting unexpected behaviour.
