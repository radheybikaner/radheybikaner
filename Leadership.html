Leadership

I co-founded Team Samvedna in 2019 to support the flood victims in Sangli and Kolhapur. I encouraged and enrolled 10 of my classmates, who have been working closely to help and assist people affected by disasters. Two of our large-scale initiatives are:

  • 2019: Sangli and Kolhapur Floods– Aggregated and distributed over 2600 kg of relief material, including food and clothing, to aid more than 580 flood affected families.
  • 2020: Led a group of 10 students in creating employment opportunities amongst Covid affected families.
  • Raised Rs 1.50 million through crowdsourcing to support:
  • Free distribution of 700 PPE Kits, 3000 N95 masks
  • Donated 7 ICU Beds to hospitals treating COVID patients.
  • Distribution of 5000 food kits for migrant workers stranded in Mumbai

PROJECT SHAAN (With Indian Development Foundation)

Created and curated Project Shaan (Self Help AatmaNirbhar- self reliant) for Indian Development Foundation, a renowned NGO with Special Consultative Status with the Economic and Social Council of the United Nations since 2012.

  • Empowering self-help group women who are Covid affected and helping them achieve financial independence by producing washable three layered cotton-polyester face masks conforming to WHO guidelines.
  • Target to produce 10 million masks, helping over 2000 women
  • The proceeds from the masks are directly transferred to the daily wager women engaged in production, offering them with earning opportunities.
